About Institute

Madhav Institute of Technology & Science (MITS), Gwalior — a beacon of technical excellence in Madhya Pradesh since 1957. Now proudly a Deemed to be University, MITS blends rich royal heritage with cutting-edge innovation to nurture future leaders in engineering, technology, and beyond.

Our Legacy

Founded by His Highness Sir Jiwaji Rao Scindia under the open door policy of the Government of India, MITS has a storied past intertwined with India's independence era. The foundation stone was laid by Dr. Rajendra Prasad, the first President of India, in 1956. The magnificent campus building was inaugurated by Dr. S. Radhakrishnan in 1964, and during its Golden Jubilee in 2008, President Dr. Pratibha Devi Singh Patil graced the occasion as Chief Guest.

Our Vision

To create world-class quality engineers and technocrats capable of providing leadership in all spheres of life and society.

Our Mission

  • Provide quality education in technical and allied disciplines.
  • Organize and promote innovative courses in Engineering and Technology.
  • Foster research, innovation, and industry collaboration.
  • Develop ethical professionals who contribute meaningfully to society.
